FAQ
What is MatchCoach?
MatchCoach is an AI dating coach and reply assistant for Hinge, Bumble, and Tinder. You screenshot a match's profile or your conversation, pick a tone, and it writes personalized replies. It also checks your draft messages for cringe and reviews your dating profile with a score and fixes. It starts free with 7 replies and needs no sign-up.
Is this safe?
Yes. Screenshots are processed securely and never stored. No account needed. Your dating conversations stay private.
Will Hinge/Bumble/Tinder ban me?
No. MatchCoach doesn't interact with dating apps at all. It reads your screenshots and you manually copy-paste replies. The dating app sees you typing normally.
How much does it cost?
Your first 7 replies are free with no sign-up or card. After that, Premium unlocks unlimited replies, profile reviews and message checks. Pricing varies by country and is shown in your local currency in the app — you can subscribe weekly or monthly and cancel anytime.
What's the difference between Android and iOS?
On Android, MatchCoach uses a floating bubble overlay that sits on top of your dating app. On iOS, it includes a custom keyboard extension so you can generate replies without leaving the conversation. Both platforms have the full in-app experience.
How is this different from Rizz AI or Keys AI?
MatchCoach lets you start with 7 free replies and no credit card (competitors ask for payment from day one), supports 50+ languages, reads up to 3 screenshots for better context, includes a message checker and profile review, and has platform-specific features like the Android floating bubble and iOS keyboard.
What languages does it support?
50+ languages including Hindi, Spanish, Portuguese, Arabic, Korean, French, German, and more. It auto-detects the language from the profile and replies in the same language.
