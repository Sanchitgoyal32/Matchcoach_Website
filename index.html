FAQ
Is this safe?
Yes. Screenshots are processed securely and never stored. No account needed. Your dating conversations stay private.
Will Hinge/Bumble/Tinder ban me?
No. MatchCoach doesn't interact with dating apps at all. It reads your screenshots and you manually copy-paste replies. The dating app sees you typing normally.
Is it really free?
Yes! MatchCoach is completely free to use. Watch a quick ad occasionally to continue, or subscribe for an ad-free experience.
What's the difference between Android and iOS?
On Android, MatchCoach uses a floating bubble overlay that sits on top of your dating app. On iOS, it includes a custom keyboard extension so you can generate replies without leaving the conversation. Both platforms have the full in-app experience.
How is this different from Rizz AI or Keys AI?
MatchCoach offers 7 free replies (competitors charge $7/week from day 1), supports 50+ languages, reads up to 3 screenshots for better context, and has platform-specific features like the Android floating bubble and iOS keyboard.
What languages does it support?
50+ languages including Hindi, Spanish, Portuguese, Arabic, Korean, French, German, and more. It auto-detects the language from the profile and replies in the same language.
Does it work for everyone?
Absolutely. Works for all genders and orientations. Upload your profile screenshots to personalize replies to your style and personality.
