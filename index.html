FAQ

Quick answers

Is this safe?
Yes. Only reads Hinge text. No photos, passwords, or other apps. All data stays on your phone. No servers, no accounts.
Will Hinge ban me?
No. The app doesn't automate anything. It suggests replies that YOU copy and paste. Hinge sees you typing normally.
Why the Accessibility permission?
To read prompts on screen automatically. Only activates inside Hinge. Reads nothing from any other app.
Is it free?
Yes! 10 free replies first session. Then watch a short video to unlock more. No subscriptions, no hidden fees.
Why not on Play Store?
Google has strict accessibility rules. We're getting approved. Meanwhile, the APK is the exact same app.
Works on iPhone?
Not yet. iOS doesn't allow reading other apps. Android only for now.
Works for girls too?
Absolutely. The AI adapts to your profile and style regardless of gender. It reads the other person's profile and crafts replies matching YOUR personality.
